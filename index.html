Add Words with Google Lens

Easily capture words from your books and add them directly to your dictation practice:

1

Install Google Lens

If you don't have it already, download the Google Lens app from your app store.

Get Google Lens
2

Capture Text

Open Google Lens, point your camera at the text in your book, and select the words you want to practice.

3

Copy to Dictation Tool

Tap the "Copy text" option in Google Lens, then paste it into the input field above.

4

Practice Dictation

Add the words and use the playback features to practice writing them as they're dictated.

Exam Preparation Guide:

  • Add challenging words in your selected language (Hindi or English)
  • Use Google Lens to capture words from books and add them directly
  • Click the play button next to each word to hear it pronounced
  • Adjust the speed to make words easier or more challenging
  • Use preset buttons for specific speeds (25, 20, 30, 35 WPM)
  • Practice writing the words as you hear them
  • Delete words once you've mastered them
